Pancakes

Pancakes are a classic breakfast favorite made from a simple batter of flour, milk, eggs, and a bit of sugar and baking powder. They are cooked on a griddle until they're golden and fluffy.

Top your pancakes with maple syrup, fresh fruits, or a dollop of whipped cream for a delicious morning treat. Pancakes are a delightful way to start your day.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
