Sandwich

A sandwich is a versatile and delicious meal made by placing various ingredients between slices of bread. It's a quick and satisfying option for breakfast, lunch, or a snack.

You can customize your sandwich with your favorite fillings, such as veggies, cheese, meats, or spreads. It's a simple and tasty choice for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Slices of Bread
  • Filling of your choice (e.g., lettuce, tomato, turkey, cheese)
  • Condiments (e.g., mayonnaise, mustard)
